Pioneering Abstraction

Abstraction exceeds the scope of aesthetics. Abstraction results from the continued development of our consciousness, rendering revolutionary technological and intellectual innovations. Digital computing encapsulates this power, which is why I leverage it in my artistic practice. Through the creation of nonrepresentational media art, I am able to experientially unify distinct disciplines and cultures within uniquely accessible sensory experiences. Join me as I utilize and develop new infrastructure for telematic creativity!
